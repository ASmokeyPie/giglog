Help & Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I register?

Click on the Register link in the navigation bar, fill out your username, email, and password, and submit the form. Make sure your passwords match!

2. How can I add a gig?

After logging in, navigate to "Add Gig", fill in the details (artist, venue, date, review), and click submit.

3. Can I follow other users?

Yes! Visit a user's profile and click the follow button. You can also unfollow them at any time.

4. How do I edit or delete a gig?

Go to your profile, find the gig you want to edit or delete, and click the corresponding button. You can only edit or delete your own gigs.

5. How do I update my profile?

On your profile page, you can update your bio and upload a profile picture. Make sure your picture is under 2MB.
